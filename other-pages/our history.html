OUR HISTORY

Our story began in 1957 in the heart of southern Italy when ICAM was founded by Cav. Pietro Paolo Bianco, for the production of metal office furniture and light shelving for storage and filing use. Today, at the helm of what is now an internationally-orientated company, there is a close-knit family group with the same ideals of their father; namely passion for their work and a clear focus on innovation and quality. Over the years technology developed by ICAM has accelerated progress in the field of intralogistics and achieved some notable accomplishments.

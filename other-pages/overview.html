Overview

We are one of theleading European manufacturersof automated systems for storage and filing. Our package includes a full range of semi-automatic mobile racking systems and automatic vertical systems either with shifting trays or rotating carriers, together with our shuttle-based ASRVs.

Through partnerships with top systems integrators and system providers we fulfil the logistic needs of thousands of companies and government agencies worldwide. In over 60 years of experience we can pride ourselves on almost 4,000 installations in 50 countries.

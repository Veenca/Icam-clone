RESEARCH AND INNOVATION

He who seeks, finds.

The future belongs to those who know how to imagine it and how to build it. At the heart of our ability to innovate lies a vision and ideas but also a fierce determination to anticipate change.

Research and innovation are the cornerstones of our strategy on technology. This is why every year we invest up to 10% of our turnover to create a working environment with the highest possible standards for employees, for business and for the community at large.

Experience, emerging technology and user-centred design are what drive our R&D operations. We never cease in our quest to develop new technology and new products and improve the performance of our existing ones.

We constantly strive to study and propose more advanced solutions in terms of:





  • Performance



  • Reliability and safety



  • Usability and Comfort



  • Cost effective




Our research knows no boundaries.
Our approach to innovation is based on the sharing of ideas and projects: to create innovation we try to follow many different paths to give everyone the chance to express their full potential.
We lean towards collaborative innovation because we are convinced that in order to keep pace with the challenges of the global market open innovation and crowd sourcing offer the chance to identify highly technological, creative solutions.
We work with leading universities, research institutes and industrial partners to tackle the technological challenges and emerging requirements in the world of automated logistics.
At ICAM we have participated in numerous research projects with some of the greatest Italian companies.

INDUSTRIAL

  • - Aerospace and Defence
  • - Oil & Gas
  • - Automotive, Mechanical, Mechatronics and Metallurgy
  • - Electrical and Electronics
  • - Textiles, clothing, footwear and Packaging
  • - Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Cosmetics and Biomedical
  • - Logistics & Transportation
  • - Industrial Automation

BUSINESS LINE
HEALTCARE

Offering automated solutions for the storage of pharmaceuticals in sealed packaging, medical devices,
surgical devices, general medical supplies and also for the filing of medical records, X-rays and
histological samples in healthcare institutions (hospital trusts, clinical laboratories etc.) and distribution companies.

  • - Hospital pharmacies
  • - Centralized local healthcare association depots
  • - Pharmaceutical distribution companies
  • - Historical archives
LEARN MORE >>

BUSINESS LINE
RETAIL

Innovative “phygital” solutions for New Omni-channel Retail omni-channel New Retail.
When used in 24/7self-service mode, they allow traditional retailers and e-tailers to automatize the
processes they use for sales, delivery / collection and return of online or offline purchases, offering
a fully integrated customer experience.

LEARN MORE >>

BUSINESS LINE
CITY LOGISTICS

Innovative “phygital” solutions for sustainable urban logistics. When used in 24/7 self-service mode, they
allow both public and private companies from various sectors, such as Public Administration, Logistics &
Transportation, Utilities, Travel & Leisure, Sharing Economy, etc., to offer a wide range of services to
sustain the urban mobility of goods and people.
